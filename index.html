📖 Game Guide & Tips

How to Play:

  1. Click the cookie to earn clicks
  2. Sell your clicks (10 clicks = 1 coin)
  3. Buy better mice to increase your Clicks Per Second (CPS)
  4. Unlock new worlds for additional boosts
  5. Complete achievements to earn CookieZP and level up!

Leveling System:

  • Each achievement earned = 100 CookieZP
  • Every 100 CookieZP = 1 Level
  • Track your progress in the cookie menu (🍪) at bottom right

Achievements:

  • 🍪 First Click - Click once
  • 💎 Coin Collector - Get 100 coins
  • 🖱️ Mouse Buyer - Buy your first mouse
  • ⭐ Click Master - Reach 10,000 clicks
  • 💰 Coin Hoarder - Collect 5,000 coins
  • 🖱️ Mouse Collector - Own 5 different mice
  • 🌍 World Explorer - Unlock 3 worlds
  • ⚡ Speed Clicker - Reach 100 CPS
